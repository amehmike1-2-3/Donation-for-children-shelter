Who We Are

A home when there is none.

SafeHaven Children's Shelter was founded with one mission — to ensure no child sleeps without a roof, a meal, or the knowledge that someone cares. We serve orphaned, abandoned, and at-risk children across the community.

  • ✦

    Safe, clean shelter with 24/7 supervision and care for every resident.

  • ✦

    Access to quality education, tutoring, and vocational skills training.

  • ✦

    Nutritious daily meals and regular medical check-ups for all children.

  • ✦

    Psychosocial support and counselling to help children heal and grow.
